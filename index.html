Welcome to StockWatch

Track your favorite stocks, analyze their performance, and manage your trade portfolio all in one place.

Features Overview

  • Trade Journal: Log your stock transactions and track your trading performance over time. Filter trades by symbol and date range for better insights.
  • Compare Stocks: Compare two stocks side-by-side using key financial metrics, such as P/E ratio, market cap, and dividend yield. This helps you make more informed investment decisions.
  • Stock Search: Easily search for individual stocks and view real-time financial data, including price, market cap, and volume. Add stocks to your watchlist to stay updated.
  • Investment Return Calculator: Estimate potential returns based on your investment amount and expected growth rate. This tool helps you project the future value of your investments.
  • Toggle View: Easily switch between table and graph views to analyze your trade data in the way that works best for you.

Start exploring the various tools to better manage your stock portfolio today!
